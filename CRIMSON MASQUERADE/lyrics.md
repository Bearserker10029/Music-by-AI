[Intro]
(A solemn pipe organ plays a funeral melody, followed by an operatic choir)
Dies irae, dies illa...
Welcome to the midnight theater.
Are you ready to cut your strings?
(High-pitched scream followed by a heavy guitar riff)


[Verse 1]
絡みつく糸、操られるまま (Karamitsuku ito, ayatsurareru mama)
Smiling through the pain, a porcelain facade
鏡の中の私は誰？ (Kagami no naka no watashi wa dare?)
We wander in the dark, lost inside this godless maze.


[Pre-Chorus]
(The bass becomes fast and punchy, a harpsichord plays in the background)
Tick-tock, the pendulum falls
堕ちてゆく、奈落の底へ (Ochite yuku, naraku no soko e)
There is no salvation in this script!
Open your eyes!
偽りの仮面を壊して！ (Itsuwari no kamen o kowashite!)


[Chorus]
(Explosion of Symphonic Metal! Double kick drum, distorted guitars, and high emotional lead vocals)
Welcome to the Crimson Masquerade!
Let the tragedy play, set the stage on fire!
血塗られた運命を舞い踊れ (Chinurareta unmei o maiodore)
No more hiding in the shadows of the night
絶望の淵から這い上がれ (Zetsubou no fuchi kara haiagare)
We are the dolls with the broken strings!
Ave, Ave, Ave Tenebrae...


[Verse 2]
(A slap bass solo transitioning into a metalcore breakdown riff)
Sweet illusions, dripping like poison
甘い毒に溺れてゆく (Amai doku ni oborete yuku)
Play the villain, play the saint, who cares?
全ては無慈悲な狂言 (Subete wa mujihina kyougen)


[Bridge (Spoken Word Interlude)]
(The music stops completely. Only the ticking of an old clock and a spectral music box can be heard. The vocalist speaks in a theatrical, almost unhinged tone)


"Ah... Did you really think you were free?
あなたの糸は決して切られていない、暗闇で見えなくなっただけ。(Anata no ito wa kesshite kirareteinai, kurayami de mienakunatta dake.)
Surrender to the eternal night!"


(A sinister laugh cut by a cymbal crash, entering the duel of solos)


[Guitar & Keyboard Solo]
(Neoclassical Power Metal musical duel: The keyboard imitating a harpsichord competing against a fast-paced electric guitar)


[Chorus]
(The chorus returns with the full symphonic orchestra supporting the melody)
Welcome to the Crimson Masquerade!
Let the tragedy play, set the stage on fire!
血塗られた運命を舞い踊れ (Chinurareta unmei o maiodore)
No more hiding in the shadows of the night
絶望の淵から這い上がれ (Zetsubou no fuchi kara haiagare)
We are the dolls with the broken strings!


[Outro]
(Heavy down-tuned guitars keeping the final beat while the operatic choir sings in the background)
Cut the strings.
世界を黒く染め上げて (Sekai o kuroku someagete)
The Requiem is over.
Amen.
(The sound of scissors snapping a string, followed by a deathly silence)
