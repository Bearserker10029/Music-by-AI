Genre: Symphonic Metal / Gothic Rock.
Vibe: Theatrical, mysterious, tragic, and overwhelming. A "midnight masquerade" atmosphere in an abandoned castle.
Instrumentation: Heavily down-tuned 7-string guitars (drop tuning), fast double bass drumming, harpsichord arpeggios, majestic pipe organ, and a very technical bass line (slap).
Structure: Includes an operatic intro and the characteristic "spoken word" (spoken interlude) that Ave Mujica always uses for their dramatic pauses.
